TicketMaster Queue Up

The queue up ticketmaster app allows its users to log in and choose from a variety of concerts that are on display. Once the user logs into the app, they get placed into a queue and based on all the people currently logging into the concert app will be plucked from the queue and allow them to purchase tickets from the artist that they want to see. This helps solve a real problem that we have seem recently with ticket platforms crashing because of high demand to see a certain artist.
